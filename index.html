About

Atrang 2.19 is freshers party events of Electronics And Communication Department @ B.P. Poddar Institute Of Management And Technology

Our purpose is to get know our juinors through cultural events

Juniors are requested to be a part of this cultural events and make this event memorable

Events

Atrang 2.19 presents both online and offline event for freshers. All information and rules are mentioned in this section

  • Glamour Hunt

    Online Event

    Closed

    Message Your Photo along with Cption and Get a chance to win Mr. / Miss. Popular


    ⬤ A participant can submit only one photo.The decision of the judges will be final .

    ⬤ The caption should be unique and within 120 characters.

    ⬤ Photo can be a selfie or a normal photo but only an individual must be present(no group photos).

    ⬤ Autolikers are strictly banned. If found guilty photo will be disqualified.

    ⬤ Likes will be counted only if both the user and liker have liked the page.

    ⬤ Caption shouldn't be copied.

  • Alfaaz Atrangi

    Online Event

    Closed

    Spill your hearts out whether it's fantasy , grievance or a love note. This event is not only for the extroverts out there but also for the introverts. The link to confess is Click Here to Go through link and write your secret message .



    ⬤ Use of foul language will not be tolerated.

  • Hash Tag

    Online Event

    Closed

    The event is simple, we give you a certain piece(picture, post, situation, etc) and you solve the glaring problem that all Instagram/ Twitter users face, YOU PROVIDE THE #HASHTAGS.Yes, as simple as that, just put an appropriate #hashtag on the piece we post and that's it.



    ⬤ No abusive words should be used.

    ⬤ Hashtags have to be either a word or a phrase. ( not more than 4 words)

    Judgement criteria -:>
